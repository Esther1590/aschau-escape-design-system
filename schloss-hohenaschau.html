Getting Here

Schloss Hohenaschau is easily accessible by car and public transport.

  • By Car: Follow signs from Aschau town center.
  • By Train: Aschau (Chiemgau) station is a 15-minute walk.
  • Parking: Available on-site for a small fee.

Accessibility

We strive to make the castle accessible to all visitors.

  • Wheelchair access to main areas

  • Assistance available upon request
